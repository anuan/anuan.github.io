Roadmap

We are currently in Phase 1 and actively seeking partners to join us.

  • Phase 1: Foundation & Proof of Concept (Now - Q2 X)
    • Goal: Secure key academic and clinical partners and initial seed funding.
    • Action: Develop the first causal model focused on a single, high-impact disease area (e.g., Type 2 Diabetes or a specific cancer subtype).
    • Outcome: A validated, non-clinical proof-of-concept demonstrating the feasibility and power of our causal approach.
  • Phase 2: Platform Development & Pilot Study (Q3 X - Q4 X)
    • Goal: Build the core platform and a functional user interface for clinicians.
    • Action: Partner with a clinical institution to launch a small-scale pilot study, gathering real-world usage data and feedback.
    • Outcome: A working prototype tested in a clinical setting, with initial data on usability and impact.
  • Phase 3: Validation & Expansion (X)
    • Goal: Rigorously validate the platform's predictive accuracy and clinical utility.
    • Action: Publish findings from the pilot study in a peer-reviewed journal and begin development on causal models for two additional disease areas.
    • Outcome: Strong evidence base to support broader adoption and a more versatile platform.
  • Phase 4: Scaling & Integration (X and Beyond)
    • Goal: Scale the platform for wider use.
    • Action: Pursue regulatory pathways (e.g., SaMD - Software as a Medical Device) and develop APIs for integration with Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems.
    • Outcome: A scalable, integrated platform ready to make a significant impact on healthcare decisions globally.
