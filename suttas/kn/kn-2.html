नागरी धम्म Nagari Dhamma

॥ नमो तस्स भगवतो अरहतो सम्मासम्बुद्धस्स ॥

खुद्दकनिकायेKhuddaka NikāyaThe Short Collection १. खुद्दकपाठपाळि Khuddakapāṭha 1. Short Passages

२. KN 2 दससिक्खापदं Das Sikkhapada Ten Precepts

Translation by Ṭhānissaro Bhikkhu.

१. पाणातिपाता वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from taking life.

२. अदिन्नादाना वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from stealing.

३. अब्रह्मचरिया वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from sensuality.

४. मुसावादा वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from telling lies.

५. सुरामेरयमज्जपमादट्ठाना वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from intoxicating fermented & distilled beverages that lead to carelessness. (intoxicants)

६. विकालभोजना वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from eating at the wrong time [after noon & before dawn].

७. नच्च-गीत-वादित-विसूकदस्सना वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from dancing, singing, music, & watching shows.

८. माला-गन्ध-विलेपन-धारण-मण्डन-विभूसनट्ठाना वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from wearing garlands and beautifying myself with perfumes & cosmetics. (beautification)

९. उच्चासयन-महासयना वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from high & luxurious seats & beds. (high seats)

१०. जातरूप-रजतपटिग्गहणा वेरमणी सिक्खापदं समादियामि ।
I undertake the training rule to refrain from accepting gold & money. (money)

सब्ब पापस्स अकरणं, कुसलस्स उपसम्पदा सचित्त परियोदपनं, एतं बुद्धान सासनं ||
